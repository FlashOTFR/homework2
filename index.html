About Me

Hello! My name is Randall Burgess and I am a student at the UW coding boot camp program. I am very excited to be taking my first foray into the coding world, and can't wait to see what's coming up for us! I have been waiting to join the coding boot camp program for almost four years, and am ready to throw everything I have at the process. I am well informed about what to expect, and am driven by the successes I've already seen come from these program, and am ready to join those happy people!

I'm not one to really talk about myself, so I'm not really certain what to put here. I have been practicing rudimentary code in preparation for the course, and I have found it very enjoyable. I do have a tech background, but in IT rather than development. I enjoy assembling my own PC's, as well as tooling around with in home media networking. I also enjoy playing music, and will absolutely one day blow up the moon.
